Estonia

After the trauma of Stalin's repressions Albert was never able to talk about the death of his mother. He could retell though that on the day of the burial it rained very heavily. He and his father had dug the grave but it flooded. Together they worked to weigh down the coffin with heavy stones.

Estonian relatives comforting the children agreed that Aliide had died in trying to save her children. However, for Albert, Stalin was to blame.
